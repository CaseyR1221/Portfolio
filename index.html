About

Me

I have put quite a bit of thought over the past few years into what I would like to do with my life, and I feel that the following purpose statement sums up all aspects of my goals accurately. I am a dedicated and hardworking young tech professional striving to acquire a comprehensive set of software development skills in order to broaden the range of projects that I may work on. I aspire to become a competent and dependable mobile developer so that I may contribute fully within my given field, and act as a fountain of information to those around me who are less knowledgeable. My personal goals aside from those pertaining to my career are to maintain a healthy and active lifestyle through regular exercise and sports, and to be happy with my appearance at all times.
