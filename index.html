Me

Hi, my name is Casey, and I am a hardworking young tech professional striving to acquire a comprehensive set of software development skills to broaden the range of projects that I may work on. I have always known that I wanted to make an impact in people lives and that I wasn’t meant to just fade into the background. I aspire to become a competent and dependable mobile developer so that I may contribute fully within my given field, and act as a fountain of information to those around me who are less knowledgeable.


When I’m not honing my development skills, I’m usually in the kitchen cooking something up. Cooking, much like software development gives me another outlet to be creative and make something awesome that I can share with others. I love trying new things out and different combinates because you never know what you might discover. My other two favorite pastimes are tennis and disc golf. I love getting outside and hitting around. It takes my mind off the more serious things so I can just enjoy myself.


I never truly knew what I wanted to do in life until now. I’m happier than I’ve ever been, and the future seems so bright. There are so many possibilities for me now that I can’t even begin to describe them. I can’t wait to see where life takes me next and what new adventure await me in the future!
